    How to get there

    Venue Map

    Almaz Convention Center, Long Biên, Hà Nội

    From Hoàn Kiếm (Guom) Lake → Almaz Convention Center

    • Taxi / Ride-hailing (Grab, XanhSM) ≈ 20–35 min off-peak • 30–60 min at rush hour This is the easiest option (especially if you are not familiar with the area). Say “Almaz Convention Center, Vinhomes Riverside,” sit back and enjoy the view.
    • Motorbike (GrabBike or personal) ≈ 20–30 min Usually the quickest through traffic.
    • Bus + short taxi ≈ 45–70 min Bus toward Long Biên / Vinhomes Riverside, then 5–10 min taxi to Almaz.
    • Driving & parking ≈ 30–60 min Parking available at Almaz / Vinhomes Riverside.

    Traffic in Hà Nội is busiest on weekdays 7:00–9:00 and 16:30–19:00. Please add a 15–20 min buffer to arrive comfortably.
